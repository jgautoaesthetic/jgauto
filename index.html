Got Questions?

Frequently asked.

Everything you need to know before booking. Don't see your question? Just give us a call or send an email.

How long does a detail take?

Most details take roughly 1–2 hours, depending on the size of your vehicle and how dirty it is. An exterior detail is typically around 1 hour, while a full interior or full detail can take closer to 2 hours.

Where do I bring my car?

Drop your vehicle off at our garage at 1760 Motorplex, Chanhassen, MN. There's plenty of parking and we'll get you on your way as quickly as possible.

How do I book an appointment?

The fastest way is through our online booking widget right on this page — pick a time and you're set. You can also call Gabe or Joseph directly, or send us an email at jgautoaesthetic@gmail.com.

What's included in each package?

Exterior ($200): Hand wash, wheels & tire dressing, exterior glass, and a streak-free finish.

Interior ($225): Full vacuum, dashboard, console, door panels, interior glass, and fabric or leather conditioning.

Full Detail ($375): Everything in both packages — the complete inside-and-out transformation.

Do you accept cash, card, or both?

We accept multiple payment methods. Reach out to us when booking if you have a payment preference and we'll let you know what works best.

What if my vehicle is extra dirty or oversized?

Heavily soiled vehicles, large SUVs, or trucks may take a bit longer and could carry an additional fee depending on the work involved. Just let us know up front when you book and we'll give you an honest quote — no surprises.

Do you offer paint correction or ceramic coating?

Right now we focus on our three core detailing packages. If you have a specific request that goes beyond what's listed, give us a call and we'll discuss what we can do for you.

What are your hours?

We're open Tuesday and Thursday after 12:30 PM, and any time on Saturday and Sunday. We're closed Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. See our full schedule above.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes — please give us at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. You can do this through Calendly using the link in your booking confirmation email, or just call us directly.
